ARENA FORGE - PHONE VERSION

Best experience: rotate your phone to landscape.

Controls:
- Left joystick: move
- Right joystick: aim and attack
- Gear: Gadget
- Star: Super (hold/aim, then release)
- Lightning: Hypercharge

Extract the ZIP before opening index.html. Progress is saved in that browser on that device.
Some iPhones block scripts opened directly from Files; a published web link avoids that restriction.
